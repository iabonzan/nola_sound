About

Founded in 2005, Nola Sound has grown steadily to become a leader in providing value-centric live concert services.
We believe that the customer should be allowed to focus on their show and let us worry about the equipment,
stagehands, and engineers. We strive to bring quality live sound to our community at a competitive rate.
